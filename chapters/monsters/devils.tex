\subsection{Lilith}
\label{sec:monster:Lilith}

Lilith, or the \emph{whore of babel}, rules over a section of hell called
babel. She rules over many legions of various devils, and is mainly
responsible for the defence of the layers of hell. Unlike other succubi she no
longer has any wings, and thus cannot fly. Like all other generals of hell,
she draws her power from directly Baphomet.
